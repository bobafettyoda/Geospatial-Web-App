# Data sources

Use both USFWS and NOAA/NMFS sources for national coverage.

- USFWS ECOS critical habitat report/mapper: active proposed and final critical habitat for FWS-listed or jointly managed species. USFWS notes its mapper does not include species solely under NMFS jurisdiction.
- USFWS Open Data: authoritative ArcGIS-hosted layers, including final critical habitat features.
- NOAA Fisheries National ESA Critical Habitat Mapper: proposed and designated critical habitat under NOAA Fisheries. The page provides application, geodatabase, map service, and metadata links.

Track source URL, access date, source update date, and source agency in the database.
